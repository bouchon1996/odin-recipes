Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • American Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Place American Cheese along with any other cheese you would like in between two pieces of bread.
  2. Place a cast iron skillet on medium-low heat and place butter in pan.
  3. Place sandwich into pan, swirling around to soak up butter and applying firm pressure.
  4. Once first side is browned and crispy, place more butter in pan and flip sandwich, again placing firm pressure until second side is browned and crispy.
