Pad See Ew

Description

Pad See Ew is a Chinese noodle dish full of sweet, salty, and umami flavors. Pad See Ew generally contains wide rice noodles, a protein such as beef, shrimp, or chicken as well as Chinese broccoli, garlic, white pepper, along with a delicous sauce commonly made with Light and Dark Soy Sauce, Oyster Sauce, and Chinese Black Vinegar. Many recipes also called for a scrambled egg as part of the dish.

Ingredients

Stir Fry Ingredients

  • Wide Rice Noodles (1 part)
  • Chicken Thighs (1 part)
  • Chinese Broccoli (1/2 part)
  • Garlic (5 to 7 cloves)
  • Whole Eggs (2 to 3)

Stir Fry Sauce

  • Light Soy Sauce (1 part)
  • Dark Soy Sauce (1 part)
  • Sugar (1 part)
  • Oyster Sauce (1 part)
  • Fish Sauce (1/2 part)
